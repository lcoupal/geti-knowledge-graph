Definitions

Courses7
The foundational learning experiences. Term 1 (gold halo): Strategies for Success, Communication and Culture, Bridging Humans and Technology, Numbers and Narratives. Term 2 (aqua halo): Quantitative Literacy, Embodied Wellbeing, Digital Tools.
Competencies21
Specific, measurable abilities students develop. Each course has 3 competencies defining what students will be able to do upon completion.
Program Outcomes7
Overarching goals: Think Critically, Navigate Digital Environments, Explore Complex Systems, Communicate Effectively, Practice Cultural Competence, Cultivate Mindsets to Thrive, Ethical Leadership.
Domains6
Broad knowledge categories: Technology & Digital Skills, Communication & Collaboration, Systems Perspectives, Analytical & Quantitative, Society/Civic/Cultural, Human Thriving.
Skills24
Industry-relevant workforce skills including Critical Thinking, Data Analysis, Communication, Collaboration, Systems Thinking, AI literacy, and more.
AI Framework4
Four dimensions: Human Insight (personal reflection), Cooperative Intelligence (human-AI collaboration), Social Awareness (equity impacts), Ethical Innovation (responsible AI use).
Principled Leadership4
Developmental framework across spheres: Self (personal integrity), Team (collaboration), Community (service/empathy), Society (innovation/public trust).
Anchor Activities5
Signature pedagogical approaches: Discovery Lab, Shared Stories, Work-in-Progress, Decoding the Discipline, Frames of Reference.
